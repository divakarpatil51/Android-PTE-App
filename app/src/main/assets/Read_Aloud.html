Strategy:

  1. You have 30 to 40 seconds to go through the text. Whisper the paragraph to yourself. Try to recognise and pronounce any difficult or long words. Understand what you are reading/saying!!!.
  2. When you speak into the microphone,
    1. Read every single word and as clearly as possible.
    2. Do not miss out words or rush and read at a moderate pace
    3. Read with meaning
    4. Try to link the words within phrases
    5. Do not emphasize grammar words such as prepositions and articles.

Scoring:

    Content: 0 to 5 points

        Do not:

  1. Misread any words
  2. Skip any words
  3. Add any words

Pronunciation:

    Point structure

  1. Native-like: Native person should understand what you are saying
  2. Advanced
  3. Good
  4. Intermediate
  5. Low
  6. Non-English

    Tips:

  1. There are 44 sounds in spoken English out of which 19 are consonant sounds. For good pronunciation you should master these 44 sounds.
  2. Word Stress: It is important that you should have proper Word stress so that machine will understand you easily

Oral fluency:

    Things to be taken care of:

  1. Rhythm: For maintaining Rhythm of sentence, intonation is important.
  2. Phrasing:
    .5 second pause after..
    • full stops (.)
    • commas (,)
    • and / or / yet / but / so
    .5 second pause before
    • which / that

Avoid following things:

  1. No hesitation - no "ummm's" or "ahhhh'ss"
  2. No False start
  3. No repetitions
