Argumentative essay

It is easy to do both side of arguments and then come to conclusion to get good marks

  1. Introduction
  2. Paragraph 1 can describe Side 1 or side 2
  3. Paragraph 2 can describe Side 1 or side 2
  4. Conclusion

Write essay sentence by sentence. Follow the below pattern:

Introduction and conclusion are exactly same

Introduction:

  • Sentence 1: Rewrite "General Statement"
  • Sentence 2: Briefly mention side 1 and 2
  • Sentence 3: Give your overall opinion

Paragraph 1:

  • Sentence 1: Restate side 1
  • Sentence 2: Give reason/s
  • Sentence 3: Give example/s
  • Sentence 4: Conclude side 1

Paragraph 2:

  • Sentence 1: Restate side 2
  • Sentence 2: Give reason/s
  • Sentence 3: Give example/s
  • Sentence 4: Conclude side 2

Conclusion:

  • Sentence 1: Rewrite "General Statement"
  • Sentence 2: Give your overall opinion on 1

Scoring:

  1. Length should be between 200 and 300 words
  2. Structure: logical structure
  3. Grammar: consistent grammar
  4. General linguistic range
  5. Vocabulary: good cmd
  6. Spelling

Time management: Total time - 20 minutes

  • Prepare = 1 minute - DON'T MISS THIS STEP
  • Write = 18 minute
  • Check = 1 minute

What to do in 1 min preparation?

  1. What are the two sides? 10 seconds
  2. Which side are you on? 10 seconds
  3. Why? agree - come up with 1/2 reason
  4. Why? disagree - come up with 1/2 reason
