Task

After reading a text, write a one-sentence summary of the passage.

Skills assessed

Listening and speaking

Prompt length

Text up to 300 words

Time to answer

10 minutes

For this item type you need to write a summary of the text in one sentence.

You have 10 minutes to write your summary. Make sure to include the main points of the reading passage in a full, single sentence of no more than 75 words.

DO NOT:

  1. include your own ideas
  2. steal whole phrases
  3. refer the passage. For e.g. do not start with "The text sayss.."
  4. use grammar u are not comfortable with
  5. use vocab u r not comfortable with
  6. include too many details!!!

DO NOT WRITE UPTO 75 words as it is not possible to write. Maximum 30 words
